Physiotherapist Degree Apprenticeship (Integrated Degree)

About the Course

Apprenticeship Code: ST0519 The Physiotherapist Degree Apprenticeship (Integrated Degree) at Liverpool Hope University offers a unique and distinctive combination of academic excellence and practical training, designed to enable apprentices to meet the requirements of the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC) and Chartered Society of Physiotherapy (CSP) as a physiotherapist. This apprenticeship has been designed to equip learners with the knowledge and skills for a successful career in physiotherapy by developing professional, innovative clinical reasoners with great communication and team working skills.

Curriculum Overview

Programme Highlights:
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: The programme encompasses all four pillars of physiotherapy, offering extensive education relevant to various practice settings, including clinical, research, leadership, educational, community, and sports environments.
  • Person-Centred Approach: Emphasis on evidence-based practice to enhance the health and wellbeing of individuals from diverse communities.
  • Professional Qualification: Graduates will be awarded a BSc (Hons) Physiotherapy and a Level 6 Apprenticeship Qualification, accredited by the CSP. Successful completion of this course means you will be eligible to apply for registration with the HCPC and CSP*.

Entry Requirements

A minimum of a Second-Class Honours degree in Computer Science, Computing, Science, or engineering-based discipline awarded by a UK university, or an equivalent higher education qualification.

International Entry Requirements

The programme is subject to approval by the University and accreditation from the Chartered Society of Physiotherapy (CSP) and Health & Care Professions Council (HCPC). Approval by the HCPC and CSP is not guaranteed. Completion of the programme will enable you to apply for membership of both the CSP and HCPC.
